Swiss Ham Sandwich

Description:

A grilled swiss ham sandwich with pickles.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Corned beef ham
  • Pickles
  • Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Butter your bread
  2. Add swiss cheese, ham, then pickles between the bread
  3. Grill on a pan at medium low temperatures, flattening with a spatula
  4. Flip after 5 minutes
